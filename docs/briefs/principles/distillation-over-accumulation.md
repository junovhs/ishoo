# Principle
Briefs should preserve distilled, reusable understanding rather than accumulate unlimited text.

# Rationale
The purpose of the Briefs layer is not to store thought, but to store compressed guidance that humans and AI can reliably retrieve and use later.

Without pressure toward brevity and structure, Briefs will decay into sludge: duplicate ideas, stale assumptions, research dumps, and padded prose.

# Implications
- brief kinds should be typed
- trusted briefs should stay small
- raw material should stay separate from project-facing guidance
- oversized briefs should split, compress, or remain drafts

# Anti-patterns
- giant thinking docs
- pasted AI reports treated as project truth
- one brief trying to cover an entire topic
- guide docs turning into wiki swamps
