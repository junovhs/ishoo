# Question
How much structure should Briefs have before they start increasing cognitive load instead of reducing it?

# Why it matters
The whole suite exists to make AI-native development feel calmer, more legible, and more deterministic.

If Briefs are too loose, they become mush.
If they are too formal, they become bureaucracy.

The product needs the minimum viable structure that creates clarity without making users feel managed by process.

# Current hypotheses
- a small set of brief kinds is enough
- most users should discover Briefs gradually
- the issue-first workflow should remain the default
- hard or soft size caps may be necessary to preserve signal

# What would resolve it
Build the first manual Briefs, use them for real work, and observe:
- whether they help future decisions
- whether they are easy to retrieve
- whether they stay small
- whether they feel adjacent to Issues or distracting from them
