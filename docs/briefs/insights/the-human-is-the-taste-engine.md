# Statement
In this suite, the human is not primarily the manual code producer. The human is the source of intent, taste, curation, and judgment.

# Why it matters
This clarifies the division of labor across the whole system.

AI can generate and iterate.
The suite can preserve structure, memory, and constraints.
The human decides what is worth making, what feels right, what is too much, and what should be kept or cut.

# Implications
- Ishoo should support prioritization and redirection
- UI should optimize for calm orientation and judgment
- Briefs should preserve conclusions the human does not want to keep rediscovering
- the suite should amplify taste and clarity rather than simulate engineering theater
